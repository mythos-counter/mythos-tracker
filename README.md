# Mythos Tracker

A simple Chakra and Points tracker for Naruto Mythos TCG matches — built to be quick to read at a glance during a game, right from your phone.

**Live app:** https://mythos-counter.github.io/mythos-tracker/

## Hi, I'm Lalo

I made this because I wanted something simple to track Chakra and Mission Points during games, without fumbling with dice or scraps of paper. Hope you enjoy the game as much as I do, and that this little tool comes in handy at your table!
